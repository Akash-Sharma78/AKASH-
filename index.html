Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Milk powder
  • 1/4 cup maida
  • 1 tablespoon ghee
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking soda

Recipe

  1. Boil 1.5 cups of milk and set aside to cool.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine milk powder, maida, ghee, and baking soda.
  3. Gradually add the cooled milk to the mixture and knead it into a smooth dough.
  4. Divide the dough into small portions and shape them into smooth balls.

Kaju katli

Ingredients

  • 200g Raw cashews
  • 150g Sugar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • Silver foil
  • 1 tsp oil

Recipe

  1. Grind: Pulse cashew in a blender into a fine powder.
  2. Sieve: Strain the powder to remove any large lumps.
  3. Syrup: Boil sugar and water in a pan until dissolved.
  4. Mix: Combine the cashew powder with the syrup and oil.
  5. Cook: Cook the mixture on low heat for 5-7 minutes until it thickens.
  6. Roll: Transfer the mixture to a greased surface and roll it out with a rolling pin.
  7. Cut: Cut the rolled mixture into diamond shapes and garnish with silver foil.
  8. Serve: Allow the kaju katli to cool completely before serving.
  9. Store: Store in an airtight container for up to a week.
